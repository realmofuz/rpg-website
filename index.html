The Realm of Uz

An upcoming MMORPG for Minecraft: Java Edition

Free a wartorn land from the grip of a tyrannical deity in this forthcoming Minecraft MMORPG, featuring an expansive storyline, a complex and highly customizable item and class system, and challenging combat mechanics that reward skill over grind.

Take the Torch.

Join the ranks of a great rebellion, and build your character with spells and items that can be specialized to any playstyle. Choose from a vast and diverse pool of spells and items to make your experience unique, and upgrade that equipment as you progress.

Find the Founders.

Delve deep into an expansive storyline with rich worldbuilding and discover secrets about the creation of the Realm. Ward off parasitic gods, uncover the truth of the three Founders and find the enigmatic Overseer looming above it all.

Dethrone the Church.

Battle your way through uniquely designed dungeons, fighting off stronger foes and leveling up as you free the Realm from the oppressive influence of the Church of Uz, not by repetition.

The World is in Your Hands Now.

Explore nine vast open regions filled with immersive content, featuring combat challenges, environmental obstacles, and more. Your journeys will reward you with special items and unique storytelling.
